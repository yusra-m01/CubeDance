CSE470 HW2: Cube Dance

Yusra Mannan 02/29/2024

Description: This program displays 9 cubes total, with 1 cube being in the center and the other 8 are surrounding it.
The center cube is stationary at the beginning while the other 8 cubes are all rotating in different directions.
The center cube will begin to rotate in the same direction as whichever cube the user has clicked nearest to.
The sliders below can adjust the rotation speed and the size of these cubes.
Furthermore, the center cube can be become stationary with the "reset cube" button and
will remain stationary until the user once again clicks dir one of the moving cubes.

Resources Used: Professor Hansford's Slides, Professor Angel's website, w3Schools
